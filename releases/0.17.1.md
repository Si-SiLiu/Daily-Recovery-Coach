## Summary

Adds a local template-based Kubios screenshot parser with three explicit page
layouts, normalized field regions, local template detection, image quality
checks, per-field preprocessing and OCR candidate voting. The bilingual review
screen highlights the active region and supports quick manual entry and local
template calibration. Final import confirmation and CSV priority are unchanged.

## Verification

Three generated synthetic fixtures pass template detection and expected field
recognition. No anonymized genuine screenshot was supplied; real-screen
accuracy is N/A and remains a release condition rather than a claimed result.
The database schema and all health calculation formulas are unchanged.

## Privacy and Safety

Recognition remains offline. Screenshots and complete OCR output do not enter
AI Context or normal logs. Unconfirmed results never write formal health data,
and originals are not deleted automatically.
