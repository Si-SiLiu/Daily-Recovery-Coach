## Added

- A macOS LaunchAgent scheduler that triggers the existing One-Click Sync
  Pipeline at 06:00 local time, with idempotent install/uninstall scripts,
  catch-up prompting, trigger provenance, and a crash-safe cross-process lock.
- Manual activity, sleep, and subjective recovery CRUD in the three domain
  pages, with explicit device-priority guidance and delete confirmation.
- Versioned canonical field resolution and persisted provenance for dashboard,
  report, and local AI Context export consumers.

## Source policy

Polar remains authoritative for overlapping measured activity and sleep fields.
Confirmed exercise type is the single manual semantic override. Kubios morning
measurements and Polar nightly measurements remain separate canonical concepts.

## Safety and privacy

No Cloud AI request is made by scheduled sync or field resolution. Raw source
rows are preserved, free-text notes remain excluded from AI Context by default,
and subjective recovery entries do not modify Recovery, Baseline, Confidence,
or Local Coach algorithms. Daily Recovery Coach is not a medical device and
does not provide diagnosis or treatment.

## Verification boundary

The LaunchAgent is installed and loaded. A real scheduled runner completed the
full Pipeline and a subsequent LaunchAgent invocation exited successfully with
`already_synced`, proving same-day deduplication. The first naturally timed
next-day 06:00 run still requires observation. macOS sleep, shutdown, or a
logged-out session can delay a calendar trigger; the app therefore offers an
explicit, non-automatic catch-up action. Polar GET reads use bounded retries for
transient TLS/network, rate-limit, and server failures.
