# Daily Recovery Coach 0.20.0

- Version: 0.20.0
- Maturity: local pre-1.0 release

## Summary

This local release replaces the separate manual exercise and sleep forms with
inline, double-click table editing and adds read-only exercise and sleep history.
Recovery is reduced to measurement time, post-waking RMSSD, and post-waking
resting heart rate, all editable in place.

Nutrition Logging 2.0 introduces eight meal-event types, actual meal time, and
normalized category items. Each allowed category stores at most five named
items with quantity and `g` or `ml` units. Breakfast, morning snack, lunch,
afternoon snack, and dinner include supplements, hydration, caffeine, and
alcohol; training fuel, bedtime fuel, and free snack use the seven requested
food categories.

## Data Safety

Migration 0.9.0 adds editable sleep and morning-recovery columns plus normalized
`meal_events` and `meal_event_items` tables. Confirmed inline exercise
corrections override only edited fields while the original Polar raw row remains
unchanged and traceable. Sleep and recovery corrections likewise preserve the
raw Polar and Kubios rows.

## Verification

The release includes CRUD, category/unit validation, five-item limits, source
resolution, raw-preservation, history projection, migration, localization, and
full regression coverage. This software remains a personal tracking tool and is
not a medical device.
