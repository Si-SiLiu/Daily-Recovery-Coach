## Summary

Adds a read-only daily prospective collection monitor. It distinguishes
`awaiting_today`, `collected_today`, `attention_required`, and
`protocol_not_started`; tracks the current streak and overdue/late records; and
surfaces the result in Pipeline output, machine governance, and Dashboard.

On protocol start day, a missing current-day record is correctly considered
on-track and awaiting collection, not overdue. No schedule was installed and no
record was fabricated or modified.

Daily Recovery Coach remains a pre-1.0 application and is not a medical device.
